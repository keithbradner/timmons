Create Your Portrait in the Pictorialist Style

William "Dever" Timmons was known for his dramatic silhouettes and high-contrast portraits. His technique emphasized:

  • Clean backgrounds - Uncluttered or solid black
  • Strong backlighting - Creating dramatic silhouettes
  • High contrast - Deep blacks and bright whites
  • Atmospheric grain - The texture of 1930s film

Step in front of the camera and we'll transform your portrait using these techniques.
